Requirements Gathering Document

Sekou School App — School Management Platform (SaaS)
Africa EdTech SaaS Multi-Role B2B
Client: Sekou (France-based)
Platform Target: Schools in Africa
BA: Atul Chauhan
Deadline: July 1, 2024
Year: 2023–24

Project Context

Client
Sekou — France-based client, platform targeting Africa
Scope
Multi-role SaaS school management system
Target Market
Schools in Africa — multi-language (English + French)
Infrastructure
Cloud (AWS South Africa); each school gets its own isolated workspace
Business Model
Freemium SaaS — 50 students free (3 months), then tiered subscription: 250–999 / 1,000–2,500 / 5,000+ students
Integrations
3rd party LMS (TBD), Social login (Google/FB), 2FA via phone
1

Platform Goals

  • Partner schools with a comprehensive educational solution that covers administrative, academic, and communication needs in a single platform.
  • Enhance administrative efficiency by digitising processes currently managed through paper registers, cash handling, and fragmented tools.
  • Enable seamless communication and collaboration among students, teachers, and administrators across school management workflows.
  • Empower students to track academic progress, engage in online learning, manage billing, and submit courses and grades digitally.
2

User Roles

●

Super Admin

Manages platform-level administration, school partner registration and verification, access permissions, and subscription management.

●

Super School Admin

Manages day-to-day school operations: teachers, students, timetables, billing, academic data analysis, and digital resources.

●

Financial School Admin

Manages billing workflows and academic data analysis — a focused subset of the Super School Admin role.

●

Teacher

Creates quizzes and assignments, provides feedback, and communicates with students and other staff members.

●

Parent

Tracks child's academic progress and billing status, and communicates directly with the school administration.

●

Student

Submits quizzes and assignments, tracks academic progress, and communicates with teachers and fellow students.

Design Q&A by Stakeholder Group

General Design Considerations All Roles

Question Client Response
Are users familiar with digital platforms today? Admins: Yes (use Sage, Odoo).  Parents: No — primarily WhatsApp / Facebook messaging.  Teachers: No (secondary schools not used to structured platforms; some university teachers adapted post-COVID).
Current mechanism for managing information? Paper (register book), cash, and Sage for accounting.
Key design constraints? Network connectivity issues; Font to be provided by client; Launch deadline July 1, 2024.

Super School Admin Admin

Question Client Response
Most important info on dashboard? Late payment notifications, professor attendance / leave requests, school name and stats.
Most frequent daily tasks? Teacher: Timetable  •  Student: Timetable  •  Admin: Dashboard (plus student registration in July / August).
School registration process? Form with: school name, number of students (250–999 / 1,000–2,500 / 5,000+), number of classes, contact person.
What does the dashboard consist of? Number of schools subscribed, number of students, number of connected users + time tracker on platform.

Financial School Admin Finance

Question Client Response
Most important info? Company turnover, late payments.
Most frequent daily tasks? Provide invoices / bills to parents, enter cash payments, send reminders to parents in arrears.

School Professors Teacher

Question Client Response
Primary daily activities? Mark student attendance, manage assignments and quizzes, communicate with students.

Students Student

Question Client Response
Primary daily activities? Check timetable, submit assignments and quizzes, track academic progress.

Parents Parent

Question Client Response
Specific challenges for parents? Reliable internet connectivity — design should consider offline capability.
Most frequent tasks? Checking fee information, child's homework, child's disciplinary status.

Module Breakdown

M1
Public

Visitor Landing Page

Public overview of the platform for schools considering signup. Covers platform benefits, school information, and a clear CTA for registration into the SaaS ecosystem.

M2
Admin

Super Admin Web App

Platform-level management: school registration and verification, subscription management, platform-wide stats dashboard, and notifications for new subscriptions, late payments, and verification requests.

M3
Public

Partner School Landing Page

Per-school public page: school presentation and information for students and parents to access the school's dedicated portal within the platform.

M4
Admin

Super School Admin Web App

Full institution management: roster management (teachers and students), timetable builder, billing (invoices, payment tracking, arrears), academic data (reports and analytics), and digital resource management.

M5
Admin

Financial School Admin Web App

Financial subset of school administration: invoice generation, cash payment entry, payment reminders, turnover tracking, and billing reports.

M6
Portal

Students Web App

Student portal: timetable view, assignment and quiz submission, academic progress tracking, and messaging with teachers and peers.

M7
Portal

School Professors Web App

Teacher portal: timetable view, attendance marking, quiz and assignment creation, student feedback submission, and messaging functionality.

M8
Portal

Parents Web App

Parent portal: child's academic progress, fee information and payment status, homework updates, disciplinary status, and direct communication with school administration.
