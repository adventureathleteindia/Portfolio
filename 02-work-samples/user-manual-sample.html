1. Introduction

The Employee Portal is your central hub for managing your employment information, viewing pay stubs, requesting time off, and updating your personal details. This guide will walk you through each feature of the portal.

1.1 System Requirements

Requirement Minimum Recommended
Browser Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+ Latest version of Chrome or Firefox
Screen Resolution 1280 x 720 1920 x 1080
Internet Connection Broadband (1 Mbps) High-speed (10+ Mbps)

2. Getting Started

2.1 Accessing the Portal

  1. Open your web browser and navigate to portal.company.com
  2. Enter your company email address in the Email field
  3. Enter your password in the Password field
  4. Click the Sign In button

[Screenshot: Login Page]

💡 Tip

Enable "Remember Me" to stay logged in for 30 days on trusted devices.

2.2 First-Time Login

If this is your first time logging in, you'll need to set up your account:

  1. Click the First Time User? link on the login page
  2. Enter your employee ID (found on your offer letter or ID badge)
  3. Enter the temporary password provided by HR
  4. Create a new password meeting the security requirements:
    • Minimum 12 characters
    • At least one uppercase letter
    • At least one number
    • At least one special character (!@#$%)
  5. Set up your security questions for account recovery
  6. Click Complete Setup
⚠️ Important

Never share your password with anyone. IT will never ask for your password.

4. Requesting Time Off

4.1 Submitting a New Request

  1. Navigate to Time Off›New Request
  2. Select the type of time off from the dropdown:
    • Vacation (PTO)
    • Sick Leave
    • Personal Day
    • Bereavement
    • Jury Duty
  3. Click on the calendar to select your start date
  4. Click on the calendar to select your end date (or same day for single day off)
  5. Enter the number of hours per day (default is 8)
  6. Add any notes for your manager in the Comments field
  7. Review the summary showing total hours to be deducted
  8. Click Submit Request

[Screenshot: Time Off Request Form]

📝 Note

Requests for 3+ consecutive days should be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance. Your manager will be notified automatically via email.

4.2 Checking Request Status

  1. Go to Time Off›My Requests
  2. View the status column for each request:
    • Pending - Awaiting manager approval
    • Approved - Request confirmed
    • Denied - Request rejected (see comments)
    • Cancelled - You cancelled the request

4.3 Cancelling a Request

  1. Navigate to Time Off›My Requests
  2. Find the request you wish to cancel
  3. Click the Cancel button (only available for Pending or Approved requests)
  4. Confirm the cancellation in the popup dialog
⚠️ Warning

Approved requests within 48 hours of the start date may require manager intervention to cancel. Contact your manager directly.

5. Viewing Pay Stubs

5.1 Accessing Pay Stubs

  1. Click Pay›Pay Stubs in the navigation menu
  2. Select the year from the dropdown filter
  3. Click on any pay date to view the full pay stub

5.2 Understanding Your Pay Stub

Section What It Shows
Earnings Gross pay, regular hours, overtime, bonuses, commissions
Taxes Federal withholding, state tax, Social Security, Medicare
Deductions Health insurance, 401(k), HSA contributions, other benefits
Net Pay Your take-home amount after all deductions
YTD Totals Cumulative earnings and deductions for the year

5.3 Downloading Pay Stubs

  1. Open the pay stub you want to download
  2. Click the Download PDF button in the top-right corner
  3. The file will download to your browser's default download folder
💡 Tip

Use Ctrl + P (or Cmd + P on Mac) to print directly from the browser.

5.4 Accessing Tax Documents

W-2 forms and other tax documents are available under Pay›Tax Documents:

  • W-2 - Available by January 31st each year
  • 1095-C - Health coverage information (if applicable)

6. Managing Your Profile

6.1 Updating Contact Information

  1. Navigate to My Profile›Contact Info
  2. Click Edit next to the section you want to update
  3. Update your information (address, phone number, emergency contact)
  4. Click Save Changes
📝 Note

Address changes may require additional documentation. HR will contact you if verification is needed.

6.2 Changing Your Password

  1. Click your name in the top-right corner
  2. Select Account Settings
  3. Click Change Password
  4. Enter your current password
  5. Enter your new password (must meet security requirements)
  6. Confirm your new password
  7. Click Update Password

6.3 Updating Direct Deposit

  1. Go to My Profile›Payment Method
  2. Click Add Account or Edit existing account
  3. Enter your bank's routing number (9 digits)
  4. Enter your account number
  5. Select account type (Checking or Savings)
  6. Set the deposit amount (percentage or fixed amount)
  7. Click Save
⚠️ Important

Direct deposit changes made after the 15th of the month will take effect in the following pay period.

7. Troubleshooting

7.1 Common Issues

Issue Solution
"Invalid credentials" error Verify you're using your company email. Check Caps Lock. Use "Forgot Password" if needed.
Account locked after failed attempts Wait 15 minutes, then try again. Contact IT if issue persists.
Page not loading properly Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Del), try a different browser, or disable browser extensions.
Missing pay stub Pay stubs are posted within 2 business days of pay date. Contact Payroll if missing after 3 days.
Time off balance incorrect Balances update after manager approval. Contact HR if discrepancy persists after approval.

8. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password if I forgot it?

A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions sent to your inbox. The reset link expires in 24 hours.

Q: Why can't I see my pay stub for the current pay period?

A: Pay stubs are typically available 1-2 business days after the pay date. If it's been more than 3 business days, contact the Payroll department.

Q: Can I access the portal from my personal phone?

A: Yes, the portal is mobile-responsive. You can also download the company mobile app from the App Store or Google Play for a better mobile experience.

Q: How far in advance can I request time off?

A: You can submit requests up to 12 months in advance. Requests for dates more than 6 months out may not be approved until closer to the date.

Q: Who approves my time off requests?

A: Your direct manager receives all time off requests and is responsible for approval. If your manager is unavailable, the request escalates to their manager after 5 business days.

Q: Can I split my direct deposit between multiple accounts?

A: Yes, you can add up to 3 bank accounts and specify a percentage or fixed amount for each. One account must be designated as the "remainder" account.
