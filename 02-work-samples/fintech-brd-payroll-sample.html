1. Executive Summary

This document outlines the business requirements for a comprehensive Payroll Management System that automates end-to-end payroll processing including employee management, salary calculations, tax deductions, direct deposits, and compliance reporting. The system will support multi-company configurations and provide self-service capabilities for employees.

Business Problem

Manual payroll processing is error-prone, time-consuming, and creates compliance risks. Organizations need an automated solution that ensures accurate, timely payroll with proper tax handling and audit trails.

Target Users

  • Payroll Administrators: HR/Finance staff responsible for running payroll and managing employee compensation
  • Company Administrators: Business owners or managers overseeing multiple companies/locations
  • Employees: Staff accessing pay stubs, tax documents, and updating personal information
  • Accountants: External or internal accountants generating reports for compliance

3. Scope & Boundaries

In Scope

  • Employee onboarding and profile management
  • Salary and compensation setup (hourly, salaried, commission)
  • Automated payroll calculation with tax deductions
  • Direct deposit and payment processing
  • Tax form generation (W-2, 1099, etc.)
  • Leave and attendance integration
  • Multi-company and multi-location support
  • Employee self-service portal
  • Compliance reporting and audit trails

Out of Scope

  • Time tracking hardware integration (Phase 2)
  • Benefits administration (separate module)
  • Recruitment and applicant tracking
  • Performance management

7. Compliance Requirements

Tax Compliance is Critical

Payroll systems must comply with federal (IRS), state, and local tax regulations. Failure to comply can result in significant penalties.

Regulation Requirement
Federal Tax (IRS) Accurate calculation of federal income tax, Social Security, Medicare withholdings
State Tax Support for all 50 states' income tax calculations and reporting
FLSA (Fair Labor Standards) Proper overtime calculations, minimum wage compliance
ACA (Affordable Care Act) Tracking for benefits eligibility based on hours worked
Data Privacy Protection of SSN, bank details, and other PII
Record Retention Maintain payroll records for minimum 7 years

8. Sample User Stories

US-001: Run Payroll
As a payroll administrator, I want to run payroll for a pay period, so that employees are paid accurately and on time.
Acceptance Criteria:
- Can select company and pay period
- System calculates gross pay, deductions, and net pay automatically
- Preview shows all calculations before processing
- Can make adjustments before final submission
- Audit trail captures who ran payroll and when
US-002: Add New Employee
As an HR manager, I want to onboard a new employee into the payroll system, so that they can be paid starting from their first pay period.
Acceptance Criteria:
- Can enter personal info, SSN, employment details
- Can set pay type, rate, and schedule
- Can configure tax withholdings (W-4 info)
- Can add direct deposit information
- Employee appears in next payroll run
US-003: View Pay Stub
As an employee, I want to view and download my pay stubs, so that I can track my earnings and deductions.
Acceptance Criteria:
- Can see list of all pay stubs by date
- Can view detailed breakdown: gross, taxes, deductions, net
- Can download as PDF
- YTD totals displayed
- Mobile-friendly interface
US-004: Generate W-2 Forms
As a payroll administrator, I want to generate year-end W-2 forms, so that employees can file their taxes and the company meets IRS requirements.
Acceptance Criteria:
- System calculates annual totals for all employees
- W-2 forms match IRS specifications
- Can generate in bulk for all employees
- Employees can access via self-service portal
- W-3 summary form generated for employer filing

9. Assumptions & Dependencies

Assumptions

  • Company has a registered EIN and state tax IDs
  • Employees have valid SSN for tax reporting
  • Direct deposit requires valid bank routing and account numbers
  • Tax tables will be updated annually before January 1
  • Internet connectivity available for cloud-based access

Dependencies

  • Payment processor integration (ACH/direct deposit)
  • Tax table provider for rate updates
  • Email service for notifications and pay stub delivery
  • PDF generation library for documents
  • Leave/attendance system for time data (optional integration)

10. Risks & Mitigations

Risk Impact Probability Mitigation
Tax calculation errors High Medium Use certified tax engine; extensive testing; regular audits
Payment processing failures High Low Backup payment processor; manual check printing capability
Data breach (SSN, bank info) High Low Encryption, access controls, security audits, SOC 2 compliance
Tax table updates missed High Low Automated update system; calendar reminders; vendor SLA
System downtime during payroll High Low High availability architecture; scheduled maintenance windows
