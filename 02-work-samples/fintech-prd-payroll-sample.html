1. Product Vision

Vision Statement

Make payroll effortless for small and medium businesses - accurate calculations, compliant tax handling, and happy employees - all in a few clicks.

Problem Statement

Small and medium businesses struggle with payroll complexity. Manual calculations lead to errors, tax compliance is confusing, and employees lack visibility into their pay. Existing solutions are either too expensive, too complex, or don't handle multi-state tax scenarios well.

Solution

A modern, intuitive payroll platform that automates the entire pay cycle - from employee onboarding to direct deposit - with built-in tax compliance, self-service employee portals, and clear audit trails. Designed for businesses with 5-500 employees across multiple states.

3. Feature Overview

3.1 Company & Employee Setup

Quick onboarding for companies and employees with guided workflows.

Key Capabilities:
  • Company setup wizard: business info, tax IDs, pay schedules
  • Employee self-onboarding via invitation link
  • W-4 and direct deposit capture during onboarding
  • Bulk employee import from CSV
  • Multi-company support from single dashboard

3.2 Payroll Processing

Run payroll in minutes with automatic calculations and preview before finalizing.

Workflow:
  • Step 1: Select pay period and review hours/salary
  • Step 2: Add bonuses, commissions, or adjustments
  • Step 3: Preview payroll with full breakdown
  • Step 4: Approve and submit for processing
  • Step 5: Direct deposits initiated, pay stubs generated
Automation: Recurring payroll scheduling, automatic overtime calculation

3.3 Tax Calculations & Compliance

Always-current tax tables with automatic federal, state, and local calculations.

Tax Support:
  • Federal income tax (based on W-4)
  • Social Security and Medicare (FICA)
  • State income tax (all 50 states)
  • Local/city taxes where applicable
  • Unemployment taxes (FUTA, SUTA)
Compliance: Automatic tax table updates, year-end W-2/W-3 generation, quarterly 941 reports

3.4 Employee Self-Service Portal

Empower employees to access their pay info and update personal details.

Employee Access:
  • View and download pay stubs (current and historical)
  • Access W-2 and other tax documents
  • Update personal info and address
  • Change direct deposit bank accounts
  • Adjust W-4 withholding elections
  • Mobile-responsive design for phone access

3.5 Reports & Analytics

Comprehensive reporting for payroll analysis, taxes, and compliance.

Standard Reports:
  • Payroll summary by pay period
  • Tax liability reports (federal, state, local)
  • Employee earnings history
  • Department/location cost breakdown
  • Year-to-date summaries
Export: PDF, CSV, Excel formats

4. Key User Flows

4.1 Running Payroll

Step Actor Action System Response
1 Admin Navigate to Payroll > Run Payroll Show pay period selection
2 Admin Select company and pay period Load employees with hours/salary
3 Admin Review hours, add any adjustments Update totals in real-time
4 Admin Click "Preview Payroll" Show full calculation breakdown
5 Admin Review preview, click "Approve & Submit" Process payroll, initiate deposits
6 System Generate pay stubs, send notifications Employees receive email notification

4.2 Employee Viewing Pay Stub

Step Actor Action System Response
1 Employee Log in to employee portal Show dashboard with latest pay
2 Employee Click on pay stub or navigate to Pay History Display list of all pay stubs
3 Employee Select specific pay stub Show detailed breakdown
4 Employee Click "Download PDF" Generate and download pay stub PDF

7. Technical Considerations

Platform Architecture

  • Frontend: React SPA, responsive design, Material UI components
  • Backend: Node.js API, PostgreSQL database
  • Infrastructure: AWS, auto-scaling, multi-region
  • Security: SOC 2 compliant, AES-256 encryption, PCI-DSS for payments

Integrations

  • ACH/Direct Deposit processor (Plaid, Stripe)
  • Tax table provider (automatic updates)
  • Email service (transactional emails, notifications)
  • PDF generation for documents
  • Future: QuickBooks, Xero accounting sync
